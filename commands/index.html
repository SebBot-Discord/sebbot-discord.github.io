COMMANDS

  NOTE: Commands with an asterisk ("*") in front of them are deprecated and may not work properly

Command Description
Seb, invite Get the link to invite Seb Bot to your server
Seb, cowsay {message} Make the cow say {message}!
*Seb, cryptic {message} Encrypts {message} in emoji!
Seb, pirate {text} Translates {text} to pirate speak
Seb, xkcd Get an XKCD comic
Seb, tell me a joke Gives you a (not so) funny joke
*Seb, search for {item} Searches for {item} on google
Seb, tell me a fact about {number} Gives you a random fact about {number}
*Seb, random meme Sends a random meme from imgflip
Seb, cat pic Sends you an adorable cat picture from HTTP-Meow
Seb, fortune cookie Reveal your true fate. The chinese are never wrong.
Seb, 8ball {question} Test your luck.
Seb, trbmb Generates a that really blank's my blank phrase
Seb, dog pic Sends an adorable picture of a dog.
Seb, be like {name} Generates a be like bill image for {name}
*Seb, firecracker Amazing display of fireworks!
Seb, yo momma Get an epic yo momma joke.
Seb, echo {msg} Seb Bot will echo whatever you want!
Seb, stats Shows the bot's status
*Seb, image {query} Searches for query with the flickr api
Seb, ping Measures the latency of the bot
Seb, gif {query} Searches for {query} on gfycat, then returns the first result
*Seb, ddg {query} Searches {query} on duckduckgo and returns the first results
Seb, xkcd Gets a random xkcd comic
Seb, news Gets the latest NY Times article
Seb, crazyimg cRaZiFys your image!
